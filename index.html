Why Hand Hygiene Matters

Hand hygiene is one of the most effective and cost-efficient ways to prevent healthcare-associated infections (HAIs). Proper handwashing and hand hygiene practices can reduce HAI rates by up to 50%. As nursing students, mastering these skills is crucial for patient safety and professional practice.

The 5 Moments for Hand Hygiene

1

Before touching a patient

Prevent transmission of germs to the patient

2

Before aseptic procedures

Prevent introduction of germs into sterile areas

3

After body fluid exposure risk

Prevent transmission of germs to other patients

4

After touching a patient

Protect yourself and prevent transmission to others

5

After touching patient surroundings

Prevent cross-contamination from equipment
